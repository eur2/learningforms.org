descriptionEn: "Through the morphological study of a common object, at first glance not defined as a living environment, the studio will question the conditions of an\_already here. The form being given, it is a question of producing an architectural project without adding or removing material, only through reading and revealing its potential as a living area: address the project not according to its use but rather as a built landscape, a space of potentiality, a practical substructure and explore its potential for occupancy. Contrary the common gesture of designing, here the only allowed operation is one of scaling."
